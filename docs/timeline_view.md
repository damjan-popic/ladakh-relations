# Timeline view

The timeline is generated from `meeting_visit` edges, which originate from the master schema columns:

- `MeetingOrVisitEntities`
- `MeetingOrVisitDates`
- `MeetingOrVisitOutcomes`

The parser extracts approximate years from strings such as `11th century`, `1042–1054`, `1625/26 onward`, or `18th–19th century`. Textual dates that cannot be parsed are retained and grouped under undated/textual records.

The timeline is for orientation. It should be treated as a structured research aid rather than a critical chronology.
